about

I am a Frontend Developer with 5 years of experience, most of them in agency (LOLA MullenLowe).

I feel comfortable in creative environments - I am used to working with Art Directors, Account Executives, dogs and Copywriters. My main objective - and biggest challenge - is keeping the original idea clean while fighting with the reality of the internet to find the best options for every project.

On a regular day I develop microsites, newsletters and animated HTML5 banners (usually served through Sizmek and DoubleClick).

On a special day I support the creative process behind awesome campaings, so awesome that many of them have even received prestigious advertisng awards in industry festivals such as Cannes Lions, El Ojo (Latin America) o El Sol (Spain).

My favourite tools are Less, Atom, Photoshop and Illustrator in a GIT environment to code safely and happily.

I love code, creativity, design and the little spaces outside of the box between them.
